Casa Gustului – Restaurant & Traditional Cuisine

Casa Gustului is a place where elegance meets authenticity, and every event becomes a memorable story. Located in the heart of Ungheni, our restaurant specializes in organizing festive events — from weddings and baptisms to corporate parties and anniversaries.

We focus exclusively on private events, which allows us to give full attention to every detail — from decoration and ambiance to the festive menu and guest experience.

For events of any size, we offer two elegant banquet halls and a spacious terrace, perfect for intimate gatherings or grand celebrations. Every space is designed with modern simplicity and tasteful refinement.

At Casa Gustului, we believe that a successful celebration is built on harmony — between delicious food, warm atmosphere, and impeccable service. Our chefs prepare a variety of dishes that blend traditional flavors with modern touches, while our staff ensures that every guest feels comfortable and welcome.

Our values: hospitality, professionalism, quality, and respect for every guest. We want every event at Casa Gustului to become a cherished memory.

Whether you’re planning a dream wedding, a small birthday party, or a corporate event — Casa Gustului in Ungheni is the perfect choice for a unique festive experience.
